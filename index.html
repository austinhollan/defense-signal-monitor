ABOUT DSM

Defense Signal Monitor is a real-time intelligence dashboard tracking 23 key defense, aerospace, and security stocks alongside military, political, and prediction market signals.

Data sources include public news, official DoD contracts releases, congressional filings, prediction markets, and open-source intelligence feeds.

Prices are pulled from Polygon.io and updated every 20 minutes during active monitoring windows.
