What is Defense Signal Monitor?

Defense Signal Monitor (DSM) tracks defense-related equities and links their performance to real-time military, geopolitical, procurement, cyber, and political events.

How are signals generated?

Signals are manually curated based on public reporting, OSINT, DoD contract awards, congressional trading disclosures, and prediction markets.

Data sources

  • Polygon.io (intraday price data)
  • Public news sources (Reuters, WSJ, etc.)
  • Open-source intelligence monitors
