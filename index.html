ABOUT DSM

Methodology, sources, and disclaimer

What this is

Defense Signal Monitor (DSM) is a real-time intelligence dashboard that tracks military and geopolitical events and maps them to defense and security stocks.

Signal types

  • MILTRACK: Military movements, strikes, deployments, casualties
  • CONTRACT: Major defense contract awards and procurement signals
  • POLITICAL: Congressional trades, budget bills, hearings, policy shifts
  • CYBER: APT activity, breaches, supply chain risk, cyber contracts
  • POLYMARKET: Prediction market probabilities on war escalation
  • OSINT: Open-source intelligence from verified monitors
  • CONFLICT: War declarations, ceasefires, regime change

Data sources

  • DoD contract awards: defense.gov
  • Market prices: Polygon.io
  • OSINT: @sentdefender
  • News: Reuters, WSJ, Politico, NPR, Bloomberg, etc.
